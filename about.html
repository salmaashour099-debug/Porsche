Our Legacy & Passion

Porsche,founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche , evolved from a design consultancy into a world-renowned manufacturer of high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and electric vehicles. Founding and Early Years Porsche was established on April 25, 1931, in Stuttgart, Germany, by Ferdinand Porsche, along with his son-in-law Anton Piëch and engineer Adolf Rosenberger. Initially, the company focused on vehicle design and consulting, rather than producing cars under its own name. Ferdinand Porsche had previously worked at Daimler-Benz and designed innovative vehicles, including early hybrid cars and the Volkswagen Beetle, which became one of the most iconic cars of the 20th centure
